This photo was taken as a still shot image in the 2013 Miyazaki film, "The Wind Rises".

The plane shown in the film is a historic replica of the early Japanese military Sho-Ri fighter, that was engineered before the dawn of World War 2.

Miyazaki's famous style of detail in his artwork shows in the wisping blades of grass and deep blue night sky.
